== Description ==

Anber Elementor Addon empowers you to create stunning and dynamic websites using Elementor. It extends your design toolkit with a collection of customizable widgets, including:

1. Anber Banner/Hero Banner: Captivate your audience with a smooth-scrolling hero section featuring compelling visuals.
2. Anber Slider/Carousel: Captivate your audience with a smooth-scrolling hero section featuring compelling visuals.
3. Anber Post Carousel : Showcase your Post in an engaging and responsive carousel format.
4. Video Popup: Lightbox your videos for a seamless and interactive user experience.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the anber-elementor-addon directory to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use the Addon widgets? =

Once activated, search for "Anber Hero Slider" in the Elementor's widget panel, widget category Anber Addon . Drag and drop it onto your page, customize the settings, and add your images or video content. Refer to the Elementor documentation for further guidance on using widgets.

= Are there any additional configuration options for the Carousel widget? =

Definitely! You can adjust the number of slides displayed, navigation settings, and autoplay behavior within the widget's settings panel.
